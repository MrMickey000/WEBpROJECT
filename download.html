    About Game

    "Subway Surfers" is a popular endless runner mobile game developed by Kiloo and SYBO Games. The game was first released in 2012 and has since gained a massive player base. The setting of the game revolves around a mischievous young character, often a teenager, who is caught spray-painting graffiti on a subway train. The player's objective is to help the character escape from the inspector and his dog by running along the subway tracks while avoiding obstacles and collecting various power-ups and coins.
